ERP project

A smart ERP system designed for schools and colleges to manage student and staff data efficiently. It simplifies everyday tasks like attendance, academic records, and HR activities, making administration more organized and less time-consuming.

Target Applications: Schools, Colleges, Educational Institutions, HR Management Systems

Features:

  • Student management including attendance, results, and records.
  • Employee and HR management for staff details, payroll, and roles.
  • Centralized dashboard for easy access to all academic and administrative data.

Air quality monitoring system

A smart system designed to monitor air quality in real time. It detects harmful gases and pollutants in the environment, helping users stay aware of air conditions and take action when needed.

Target Applications: Smart Cities, Environmental Monitoring, Industrial Safety, Indoor Air Quality Tracking

Features:

  • Detects pollutants like CO2, smoke, and toxic gases accurately.
  • Provides real-time readings with alert notifications.
  • Compact and efficient design for continuous monitoring.

Data logger

A practical data logging system that records and stores information over time. It is useful for tracking parameters like temperature and humidity, making it ideal for monitoring and analysis purposes.

Target Applications: Industrial Monitoring, Research Labs, Weather Tracking, Equipment Diagnostics

Features:

  • Records data continuously with accurate timestamps.
  • Easy access to stored data for analysis and reporting.
  • Supports multiple sensors for versatile usage.

Weather station

A compact weather monitoring system that measures environmental conditions like temperature, humidity, and pressure. It provides real-time insights useful for understanding local weather patterns.

Target Applications: Agriculture, Climate Studies, Educational Projects, Personal Weather Tracking

Features:

  • Tracks key weather parameters in real time.
  • Accurate sensor-based readings for reliable data.
  • Simple interface for easy monitoring and display.

Battery alarm system

A simple yet useful system that monitors battery levels and alerts users when the charge drops too low. It helps prevent unexpected shutdowns and ensures timely action.

Target Applications: UPS Systems, Electric Vehicles, Backup Power Systems, Consumer Electronics

Features:

  • Detects low battery levels and triggers alerts instantly.
  • Compatible with different types of batteries.
  • Energy-efficient and reliable performance.
