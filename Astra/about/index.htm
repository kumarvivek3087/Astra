Astra Embedded & AI technologies LLP

ASTRA Embedded and AI Technologies LLP is a technology-focused company delivering innovative solutions in Embedded Systems, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Machine Learning (ML). We are committed to bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world industry requirements through practical training, product development, and consultancy services.
We specialize in embedded software development and industry project consultancy, helping students, startups, and organizations build reliable and scalable solutions. Our expertise covers microcontroller-based systems, IoT applications, real-time data processing, and intelligent automation.
Along with consultancy, ASTRA offers industry-oriented training programs in Embedded Systems, AI, and ML. Our courses are designed with a strong focus on hands-on learning, real-time projects, and practical implementation, enabling students to gain the skills required for modern engineering careers.
We also develop innovative products such as:
 • ERP Solutions
 • Weather Monitoring Systems
 • Air Quality Monitoring Systems
 • Data Loggers
These solutions are designed to meet industrial, environmental, and automation needs with accuracy and efficiency. At ASTRA, we believe in innovation, quality, and skill development. Our goal is to empower learners and organizations to succeed in the rapidly evolving world of embedded and intelligent technologies.

Astra Embedded and AI technologies LLP's mission is to make technical education accessible, practical, and career-oriented, equipping students with the skills to thrive in a competitive global market.

Our Vision

To nurture future leaders and innovators by providing hands-on, entrepreneurial technical education and empowering India’s youth and expanding our impact on a global scale.

Our Mission

To make technical education practical and accessible, empowering student innovation and leadership across India and beyond, fostering a generation of globally competitive technologists and entrepreneurs.
