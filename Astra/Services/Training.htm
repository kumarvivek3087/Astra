At Astra Tech, our Training & Skill Development Programs are designed to help students and professionals move beyond theoretical learning and gain practical, hands-on experience.

We focus on building real skills that are relevant to today’s fast-changing technology landscape. Through interactive sessions, practical exercises, and real-world case studies, learners develop a deeper understanding of concepts and how they are applied in professional environments.

Our Services

01

Hands-on Workshops in Embedded Systems, IoT, and Programming
  • ● We conduct practical workshops where participants learn by building and experimenting with real systems.
  • ● These sessions focus on understanding core concepts while applying them through guided hands-on activities.

02

Faculty Development Programs (FDPs)
  • ● Our FDPs are designed to help educators stay updated with modern technologies and teaching approaches.
  • ● We provide technical training and practical insights that faculty can bring back to their classrooms and labs.

03

Corporate Training and Customized Technical Sessions
  • ● We offer specialized training programs for organizations looking to upgrade the technical skills of their teams.
  • ● Each session is tailored to match the company’s technology needs and learning objectives.

04

Lab Setup Consultation and Curriculum Support
  • ● We assist institutions in designing and setting up technical labs that support effective hands-on learning.
  • ● Our team also helps structure practical curricula that align with current industry standards.
